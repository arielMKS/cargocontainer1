ABOUT US

PCM is North America’s largest full-service supplier to the intermodal industry, providing depot services, equipment sales and transportation services. PCM provides equipment repair, maintenance, storage and redistribution services to global shipping lines and leasing companies. PCM is also a leading retailer of standard and modified equipment in new and used condition to consumers, corporations, and government and military institutions.


PARTNER WITH US

PCM is fortunate to work with the best in the business. We have partnerships with designers, architects, brands, and businesses to continue to push the limits as to what you can do with a shipping container.
